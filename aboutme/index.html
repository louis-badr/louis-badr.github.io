about me

Hi! My name is Louis, I am a 24 years old creative technologist and maker based in Paris.


I am coming to the end of my studies at the Leonardo da Vinci Engineering School (ESILV) where I am pursuing a Master's degree at the DeVinci Innovation Center / Institute for Future Technologies. The program is focused on innovation, research and manufacturing on the format and in collaboration with the MIT MediaLab.

I do a bit of everything from CAD, programming, electronics, to fabrication for use in research or artistic projects.

I am currently working on and interested in the fields of HCI, haptics and neuroscience. :)

You can contact me at: contact@louis-badr.fr
