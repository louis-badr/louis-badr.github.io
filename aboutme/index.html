about me

Hi! My name is Louis, I am a creative technology engineer and maker based in Paris.

I recently graduated from the Leonardo da Vinci Engineering School (ESILV) in Paris - La Défense, where I obtained a Master's degree from the DeVinci Innovation Center / Institute for Future Technologies. The program focused on innovation, research, and manufacturing, on the format and in collaboration with the MIT Media Lab.

I do a bit of everything from CAD, programming, electronics, to fabrication for use in engineering, research and artistic projects. I am currently working on and interested in the fields of HCI and haptics. :)

You can contact me at: contact@louis-badr.fr
