about me

Hi! My name is Louis, I am a creative technology engineer and maker based in Paris.

I have recently graduated from the Leonardo da Vinci Engineering School (ESILV) where I obtained a Master's degree at the DeVinci Innovation Center / Institute for Future Technologies. The program is focused on innovation, research and manufacturing on the format and in collaboration with the MIT MediaLab.

I do a bit of everything from CAD, programming, electronics, to fabrication for use in research or artistic projects. I am currently working on and interested in the fields of HCI and haptics. :)
